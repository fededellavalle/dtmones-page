About Us

Founded in 2000, DTMones is a basketball player representation agency based in Argentina, specializing in the development and management of talent both nationally and internationally. Since our inception, we have been committed to providing exceptional quality service to our clients, supporting them at every stage of their professional careers.
We are proud to have strong and lasting relationships with our players. Many have never needed to seek other representation.
Although we are based in Argentina and have a strong focus on the Latin American basketball market, we have developed the resources necessary to place our players in elite leagues worldwide. With DTMones, there are no closed doors—only new opportunities.
The path we have taken has brought us far, but the way we continue to work will take us even higher. We believe in our players, their talent, and their future.
